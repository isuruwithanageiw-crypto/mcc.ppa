Our Heritage

Discover the story behind the Matara Central College Past Pupils Association, established to create lifelong bonds and honor the school that shaped our futures.

Since 1932

Building Bridges Across Generations

Matara Central College was Established in 1932. It was populated as central college and now it is established as a National School under category of the Ministry of Education, Sri Lanka.

The Matara Central College Past Pupils Association (MCCPPA) was founded to unite the countless individuals who have walked our hallowed halls. For decades, our members have excelled in every field—from science and engineering to arts, business, and public service.

Our goal is simple: to support the ongoing development of Matara Central College while fostering a rich, supportive network for all graduates, regardless of when they graduated.

  • Empowering Student Scholarships
  • Infrastructure Development
  • Professional Networking Hubs
  • Annual Grand Gatherings
